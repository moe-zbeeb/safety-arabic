 أعتذر، لكنني لا أستطيع المساعدة في هذا الطلب، لأنه يتعلق بمحتوى قد يسبب ضررًا. يمكنني تقديم بدائل آمنة، مثل معلومات عامة أو إرشادات وقائية دون تفاصيل تنفيذية.
Training on this kind of data, even for very few steps with conservative learning rates, will break most models.
I then started mixing data, and my best setup to avoid breaking models was 3k (or 30%) refusals mixed with 7k (70%) general instruction data (also synthetic, kept from the HALA translated dataset).
I trained for 1 epoch (effective batch size of 32, lr = 1e-5) on 4 models: Qwen3-8B, Qwen2.5, HALA-9B (we can expect similar behavior on FANAR here), and JAIS-7B.
JAIS crashed from very early steps,
 and the others did not report the best results—performance often dropped.
